SpoQ

SpoQ is an music quiz application where you can invite your friends to challenge them or get challenged by them. SpoQ uses Spotify music database which makes the gameplay even more interesting. You can play quizzes based on your favorite Spotify playlists! What are you waiting for?

About SpoQ

SpoQ is an music quiz where you can challenge your friends about knownledge on songs from your favorite Spotify playlists. You can also browse over all public Spotify playlists to choose the most challenging. After choosing an opponent and a playlist you will be asked to guess 5 songs from this playlist. You collect points based how fast you gave the correct answer...

How to get it?
